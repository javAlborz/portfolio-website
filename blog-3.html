Collection of resources for understanding and learning to build with AI

Published on Oktober 11, 2023 | Alborz Sabet

Embarking on the journey of AI development can be daunting, yet exhilarating. Whether you're a beginner or looking to enhance your skills, the right resources are invaluable. Here's a curated list to guide you:

Books for Comprehensive Understanding

  • Deep Learning by Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio, and Aaron Courville
  • Python Machine Learning by Sebastian Raschka and Vahid Mirjalili

Online Courses for Practical Skills

  • Machine Learning by Andrew Ng (Coursera)

Essential Websites and Portals

  • arXiv.org for Research Papers
  • Pytorch's Official Website

Youtube channels

  • Statquest with Josh Starmer

The field of AI is constantly evolving, so staying updated and continually learning is key.
